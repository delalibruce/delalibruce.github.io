poetry out loud

my creative process

poetry out loud means so much to me. in a lot of ways poetry, especially verbal delivery, is a dying art. that is what poetry out loud is all about - bringing the words of poets to life with one's own voice. much of my creative process to prepare for these competitions was searching through the entire anthology of poems, choosing a select few that felt compelling to me, and then dissecting the poem stanza by stanza, line by line, word by word to understand the nuances and choices of the poet and how to best convey that in my delivery.

highlights throughout the years i was involved in this include performing on the senate floor of the California state capitol and representing California in the national competition as the 2021 Calfornia state champion.
