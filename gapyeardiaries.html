gap year diaries

my creative process

i've been making "vlogs" since i was young. after graduating high school, i set off to europe for my gap year and documented the process through film. i resolved to capture each experience and emotion in order to encapsulate the endeavor and hone my storytelling skills. getting footage was always super easy, editing is really where the creative challege lies. each video took me 20+ hours to edit.

i posted these videos publically for friends and family. one surprising + rewarding aspect of this project was that these videos reached and impacted strangers who were also gapping or curious about taking one.

rationale for my gap year.

tools: canon g7x mark ii, iphone 11 pro, final cut pro x, procreate
