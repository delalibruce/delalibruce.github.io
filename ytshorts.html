my creative process

editing my gap year diaries was a laborious process, each video taking 20+ hours to finish. the desire to craft all of the footage into a coherent and compelling story would often lead to a crippling creative block.

recently, i've been creating these short videos to capture the spirit behind my travels and adventures with friends. with these, the pressure is off and i get to exercise my creative muscle! win win.

tools: final cut pro x
