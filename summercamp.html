summercamp

hosting a no-tech retreat focused on bonding and childhood nostalgia for Stanford founders + builders

Design Challenge

in many ways, this project is an extension of hot takes. most retreats for founders and builders are designed to provide space for intense, focused work. rana, molly, and i wanted to experiment to see if we could design something that was instead wholly focused on forming authentic connections between young student founders and builders.

Solution

a no-tech retreat for Stanford student founders focused on bonding and childhood nostalgia.

Details

as with our hot takes event, we designed every aspect of this retreat with intentionality towards our design vision. read more about it here. stay tuned for more retreats like this!
